## Tips for Best Results

1. **Keep it cute** - Even destruction should be adorable
2. **Use bright colors** - Pastels and neons
3. **Chibi proportions** - Big heads, small bodies
4. **Add tiny details** - Fleeing people, tanks, helicopters
5. **Movie references** - Film strips, posters, marquees
6. **Atomic imagery** - Glowing effects, radiation symbols
7. **Japanese aesthetic** - Neon signs, Tokyo architecture
8. **Monster variety** - Include Mothra, Rodan, etc.
9. **Use --s 250** for stylized pixel look
10. **Use --v 7** for game art style
