## Tips for Best Results

1. **Always include fireplace/fire element** - Central to cabin coziness
2. **Show snow outside** - Contrast between cold outside, warm inside
3. **Use warm wood tones throughout** - Knotty pine, weathered planks
4. **Add plaid/flannel touches** - Blankets, cushions, curtains
5. **Include comfort items** - Hot drinks, books, blankets
6. **Natural materials** - Stone, wood, wool, leather
7. **Vintage/rustic details** - Oil lamps, cast iron, handmade items
8. **Mountain views** - Windows should show snowy landscapes
9. **Use --s 250** for more stylized pixel art look
10. **Use --v 7** for game art style
