## Tips for Best Results

1. **Warm lighting always** - Fireplaces, candles, magic orbs
2. **Add magical touches** - Floating objects, sparkles, glowing runes
3. **Include creature companions** - Familiars add life and charm
4. **Round shapes = cozy** - Round windows, doors, furniture
5. **Nature integration** - Vines, flowers, living wood
6. **Use --s 250** for more stylized pixel art look
7. **Use --v 7** for anime/game art style
