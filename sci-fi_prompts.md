## Tips for Best Results

1. **Scale matters** - Include massive ships, tiny fighters for contrast
2. **Danger everywhere** - Battle damage, explosions, warnings
3. **Lived-in universe** - Wear, tear, history visible
4. **Light vs dark** - Rebels warm, Empire cold color temps
5. **Stakes are real** - Death, loss, sacrifice visible
6. **Use --s 250** for more stylized pixel art look
7. **Use --v 7** for dramatic cinematic quality
