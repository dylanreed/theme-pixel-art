## Tips for Best Results

1. **Embrace the grime** - Cyberpunk should feel lived-in, not sterile
2. **Contrast is key** - Warm interior lights vs cold neon outside
3. **Rain adds mood** - Include rain reflections for authenticity
4. **Mix high and low tech** - Old CRTs next to holograms
5. **Use --s 250** for more stylized pixel art look
6. **Use --v 7** for anime/game art style

## Color Palette Suggestions

Cyberpunk signature colors:
- **Neon Pink**: #FF00FF, #FF1493
- **Cyan/Teal**: #00FFFF, #00CED1
- **Hot Orange**: #FF4500, #FF6600
- **Electric Blue**: #0080FF, #00BFFF
- **Sickly Yellow**: #FFFF00, #FFD700 (for signs)
- **Dark Base**: #0a0a0f, #1a1a2e, #16161a
- **Chrome/Metal**: #708090, #A9A9A9
- **Smog/Pollution**: #4a4a4a, #696969
