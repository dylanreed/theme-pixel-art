## Tips for Best Results

1. **Caustic lighting** - Include light ray patterns filtering through water
2. **Bubbles everywhere** - Adds life and movement
3. **Bioluminescence** - Natural lighting source for deep scenes
4. **Mix scales** - Tiny fish near camera, whales in distance
5. **Color shifts with depth** - Shallow = warm colors, deep = blue/green only
6. **Use --s 250** for more stylized pixel art look
7. **Use --v 7** for anime/game art style
