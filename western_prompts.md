## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use warm orange/brown palette** - Sunset tones throughout
2. **Add dust/haze effects** - Dusty atmosphere is key
3. **Include western props** - Wanted posters, whiskey, cards
4. **Show dramatic skies** - Sunsets, storm clouds, stars
5. **Weather the textures** - Everything is sun-faded and worn
6. **Use bullet holes sparingly** - Adds character
7. **Include silhouettes** - Classic western composition
8. **Desert plants** - Cacti, tumbleweeds, sparse vegetation
9. **Use --s 250** for stylized pixel look
10. **Use --v 7** for game art style
